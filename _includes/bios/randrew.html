Rachel Andrew

Rachel Andrew is a front and back-end web developer, speaker, author/co-author of 22 books including The New CSS Layout, and a regular contributor to a number of publications both on- and offline. She is also co-founder of the CMS Perch, Editor in Chief of Smashing Magazine, a Google Developer Expert, and an Invited Expert to the CSS Working Group. She writes about business and technology at rachelandrew.co.uk.
