Accessibility Training

Professional development from the accessibility expertise of Aquent Dev6 and Gymnasium.

  • How to Improve Your Website Through Accessibility Testing (Part 1)

    In this live coding tutorial, Michael Vano of Dev 6, and Jeremy Osborn, the Academic Director of Aquent Gymnasium, will demonstrate how you can use the free Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool (WAVE) to perform a series of automated tests in order to improve the accessibility of a website.

  • How to Improve Your Website Through Accessibility Testing (Part 2)

    In this live coding tutorial, Michael Vano of Dev 6, and Jeremy Osborn, the Academic Director of Aquent Gymnasium, will demonstrate four common techniques for manually checking (and then fixing) accessibility issues on your websites.
