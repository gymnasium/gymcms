Welcome to Gymnasium

Registering with Gymnasium gives you access to all of our current and future free courses. Not ready to take a course just yet? Registering puts you on our mailing list - we will update you as courses are added.

Next Steps

As part of joining Gymnasuim, you will receive an email message with instructions for activating your account. Don't see the email? Check your spam folder and mark Gymnasiums emails as 'not spam'. At Gymnasium, we communicate mostly through email.

Need Help?

Need help registering with Gymnasium? View our FAQs for answers to commonly asked questions. You can find the answers to most of your questions in our list of FAQs. After you enroll in a course, you can also find answers in the course discussions.
