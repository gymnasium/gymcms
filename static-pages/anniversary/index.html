10 Year Anniversary

Design a career you love with free online courses on design, development, UX, accessibility, and career skills from industry experts.

2012

Gymnasium Summer of Learning Pilot

Aquent shows the creative community some love during the Summer of Learning in 2012 by providing creative professionals classes on HTML5 and career skills.

Related Content

  • Summer of Learning YouTube Channel
  • Real Life: MOOCs Filling Private-sector Skills Gaps

2013

Gymnasium Official Launch (Version 1)

Aquent Gymnasium releases two free certificate online courses, Coding for Designers and Responsive Web Design (both retired now) in July 2013 for another summer of free online learning.

2014

Gymnasium Gym Shorts

Aquent Gymnasium releases the short course format Gym Shorts, learning within an hour or less, with Defeating Busy with Brett Harned, teaching you how to estimate, plan, track, and manage your time to complete projects.

2015

Gymnasium Relaunch on Open edX (Version 2)

Aquent Gymnasium relaunches in November 2015, built on the Open edX platform, developed by MIT, Harvard, and edX.

2015

Live Coding on Twitch

Aquent Gymnasium livestreams (former) Director of Technology Mike Bifulco’s weekly live, in the wild, open development on Twitch in December 2015 — available on the Gymnasium YouTube channel.

2016

Gymnasium Release Webinars

Aquent Gymnasium releases their first of many webinars in April 2015, Designing for Understanding with Stephanie Hay and John Hodgins.

Related Content

  • Gymnasium Webinars

2018

Gymnasium Launch Badges for Gym Shorts

Aquent Gymnasium launches badges for Gym Shorts with the release of Brad Frost’s course Working with Atomic Design and Pattern Lab in July 2015.

2018

Gymnasium Win Elevate Award

Aquent Gymnasium wins the American Staffing Association’s first-ever Elevate Award in October 2018, honoring the most outstanding work-based learning programs in the staffing and recruiting industry.

2018

Gymnasium Release Take 5 Tutorials

Aquent Gymnasium releases Take 5 tutorials, a five-minute video-based course format for learning design, development, UX, accessibility, and career skills from industry experts, continuing their mission to provide free online learning to anyone, anywhere in the world.

2020

Gymnasium Win Elevate Award, Again

Aquent Gymnasium wins a second American Staffing Association Elevate Award in September 2020, honoring the most outstanding work-based learning programs in the staffing and recruiting industry.

2022

Gymnasium Launch Portfolio Workshop Pilot

Aquent Gymnasium launches its live, real-time online Improving Your Portfolio workshop.

2023

10 Years of Free Online Learning

Aquent Gymnasium celebrates 10 years of providing free online learning with courses, tutorials, articles, and webinars on design, development, UX, accessibility, and career skills from industry experts to anyone, anywhere in the world.
