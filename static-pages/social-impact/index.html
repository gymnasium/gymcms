Social Impact

In addition to lifelong learning, we at Gymnasium care about using our platform to support marginalized communities. From closing the gender gap in tech to creating job opportunities, we believe that diversity is a feature, not a bug. Here’s a collection of causes we care about that we’d love for you to consider supporting — whether that looks like a follow on social media to increase their exposure or a financial donation.
